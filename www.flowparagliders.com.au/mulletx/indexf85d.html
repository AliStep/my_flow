The MulletX marks a major advancement in parakite technology for us. Based on the Mullet, the MulletX is designed for a faster and more dynamic flight experience. Its is faster and its design gives more dive, more energy conversion and a more extended flare. Making it the perfect choice for pilots who crave a responsive and high-energy glider.

One of the standout features of the MulletX is its ability to convert kinetic energy into altitude with exceptional precision and easiness. This allows for smooth and powerful altitude recovery after each maneuver, enabling pilots to effortlessly maintain control and adjust their course as needed.

Unlike the Mullet, which offers a choice between standard and pro risers, the MulletX features a single riser option only. The MulletX offers more dive and is is faster in comparation to the orignal Mullet. The result is:  increased speed, more dive and better energy retention.

For those used to the original Mullet’s performance in lighter wind conditions while soaring cliff faces on the coast it’s important to note that the MulletX performs better when scaled up by one or two sizes. This allows pilots to fully harness the MulletX’s potential, as the glider is faster and has a different airfoil that requires more wind to stay aloft. However, for mountain flying and speed flying, scaling up is not necessary.

In conclusion, while both the Mullet and the MulletX offer distinct flying experiences suited to different needs and styles, the MulletX in its esence has can give higher speed, more dive capability, and energy conversion.

The original Mullet, on the other hand, remains a versatile and reliable choice for efficient coastal soaring and controlled three-dimensional flight. Regardless of choice pilots can expect an exciting experience.

It’s also worth noting that if a more relaxed flight is desired, the MulletX is fully capable of providing that. While it offers high-speed dives and additional energy when needed, it can be as tame and accessible as the original Mullet, transforming into a turbo-charged racing machine only when the pilot desires it.

Whether you’re in the mood for a relaxed enjoyable flight or seeking the most thrilling paragliding experience with cutting-edge parakite technology, the MulletX offers it all. The choice is in your hands – what do you want to get out of your flight?

REFLEX SHARK NOSE

The MulletX’s Reflex profile is one of the key performance innovations behind its exceptional handling and safety. We designed this profile to provide pilots with the confidence and security needed for high-speed dives, ensuring they can push the glider to its limits without ever worrying about collapse.

The Reflex profile is the strongest in our entire range and its performance is nothing short of remarkable. Even in natural, coastal laminar airflow, we have been unable to induce a collapse no matter how forcefully we attempt it. This resilience speaks volumes about the profile’s inherent safety and collapse resistance, giving pilots the peace of mind to explore the full potential of their glider with complete trust.

3D Cut Optimization and Advanced sail tensioning

The MulletX is built to be a highly robust glider, with its internal geometry meticulously engineered to provide optimal structural support without adding unnecessary weight. Every panel and diagonal have been precisely calibrated and analyzed using advanced computer modeling to strike the perfect balance between chord and span-wise sail tension. This level of precision minimizes spanwise distortion, resulting in a canopy that feels consistently taut and responsive.

In selecting materials, we carefully studied all options available and chose the taut, long-lasting Porcher Skytex 38 for its exceptional durability and performance. This material was chosen for its ability to maintain tension and shape over time, ensuring a canopy that stays responsive flight after flight.

The sail’s flex characteristics have been finely tuned to offer exceptional feedback, essential for pilots to accurately sense the air around them. A well-tensioned sail is crucial for providing the precise, immediate feedback needed to interpret subtle changes in lift and sink—particularly when flying in dynamic or turbulent conditions. This heightened sensitivity allows pilots to “feel” the air, making it easier to respond to fluctuations in lift and navigate through challenging conditions with confidence and control.

ARC

The MulletX arc is a key factor behind the glider’s exceptional agility, providing effortless and precise roll control. This design feature allows for a smooth, controlled dive and impressive barrel authority, giving pilots the confidence to push their limits.

Through extensive testing and multiple prototypes, we meticulously refined the arc to achieve the perfect balance between straight-line performance and dynamic turning. The result is a glider that not only excels in precision and responsiveness but also inspires pilots to explore its full potential in a safe and exhilarating way.

LINES

The MulletX lines are made from a carefully selected combination of low-drag materials, offering the best balance of strength-to-diameter ratio in the industry. We utilize both sheathed and unsheathed lines to ensure maximum performance and durability.

When selecting the right lines for the MulletX, our primary focus was minimizing the risk of knotting prior to inflation. This attention to detail ensures that every flight begins smoothly, with the lines performing flawlessly and without compromise.
