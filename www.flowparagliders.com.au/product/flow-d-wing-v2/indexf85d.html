FLOW D-WING V2

 

Redefining Stability, Range and Upwind Performance

The Flow D-Wing V2 represents a major evolution of our original D-Wing concept, bringing significant advancements in stability, control, usable range and most notably, upwind performance.

At the heart of the D-Wing V2 is our reflex airfoil design, delivering great stability and allowing the wing to be fully depowered without the risk of collapses. This enables the D-Wing V2 to point high upwind across an wide range of conditions. From underpowered, marginal foiling sessions to fully overpowered riding in gusty winds flying on the “A” lines only.

Riders can confidently sail upwind with a stable, clean canopy, maintaining control and efficiency even when significantly overpowered. The result is safer handling, easier stow and relaunch and consistently strong upwind angles.

Bigger Range. More Stability.

With the V2, we pushed the concept much further. The wing is noticeably more stable and offers a smuch wider usable range, with a refined top end and a great low end for early “up-on-foil” takeoffs. Control in overpowered conditions has been greatly improved, making the D-Wing V2 easier to manage, jibe and turn while still maintaining upwind efficiency.

When tacking upwind, the wing can be completely depowered and flown using only the front lines, effectively shedding excess power while still holding a strong upwind line. This defining characteristic sits at the core of the D-Wing V2’s design philosophy.

Improved Handling and Playfulness

One of the most noticeable improvements of the D-Wing V2 is how intuitive and playful it feels. Turning and jibing are smoother and more predictable, with riders consistently reporting fewer falls compared to the V1. The result is longer sessions, increased confidence and faster progression.

New Bar Design

The D-Wing V2 introduces a cleaner, more refined full-carbon bar. A key update is the floating B-attachment point, allowing the back hand to move freely along the bar. This significantly improves ergonomics, comfort and overall control, especially when riding powered or pushing upwind angles.

Faster, Cleaner Stow and Relaunch

Stowability has been significantly improved with shorter, approximately arm-length lines, making it easier to manage the canopy and run hands through the lines quickly and cleanly.

Split A-lines clearly identify the front lines, further simplifying stow and redeploy procedures. The entire system –  canopy, lines and bar – remains fully colour-coded, reducing tangles and making relaunches faster and more reliable.

Smaller Pack Volume. Premium Materials.

Despite using a durable 32-gram fabric, the D-Wing V2 packs down significantly smaller thanks to a new premium ripstop material that maintains durability while greatly improving packability.

In true Flow fashion, only top-tier materials are used throughout – from the canopy fabric to the carbon bar components and high-quality line choices – delivering an ideal balance of durability, weight and real-world performance.

The Next Step in Parawing Evolution

The Flow D-Wing V2 is more than a refinement – it’s a clear step forward. With outstanding upwind ability in both underpowered and overpowered conditions, increased stability, a wider usable range and more playful handling, the D-Wing V2 sets a new benchmark for modern parawing design.

Flow D-Wing V2 Bar

FLOW D-WING V2 BAR

REFINED CONTROL, CLEANER ERGONOMICS, BETTER ON-WATER-FEEL

The Flow D-Wing V2 introduces an updated bar design, delivering a cleaner feel and improved functionality on the water.

The V2 bar removes the traditional B lines, giving your hand complete freedom to move naturally along the bar. A redesigned overhanging section allows you to comfortably hook two or three fingers, improving grip, control and overall ergonomics, especially in dynamic riding conditions.

Built from full carbon fiber, the bar is both lighter and stronger, offering a precise and responsive feel without added weight. The brake line attachment has also been refined with a streamlined, low-profile connection, helping lines release cleanly if they momentarily catch during jibes or maneuvers.

This is not a minor tweak, but a meaningful upgrade that improves handling, reduces interference and enhances control. The result is a smoother, more intuitive experience and greater confidence every time you’re on the water.

Flow D-Wing Stash Belt

FLOW D-WING STASH BELT V2

SMARTER STOWAGE, BETTER FIT, MORE FREEDOM

The Flow D-Wing Belt V2 is an essential accessory for parawinging, designed to give you complete freedom of movement on the water. With its integrated stretch pouch, it allows you to easily stow your parawing and free your arms, so you can focus fully on foiling and riding swells downwind.

The updated D-Wing Belt V2 takes this concept further with a larger, more user-friendly pouch, making it significantly easier to pack your parawing in and take it back out, even while on the water. Stowing and redeploying your wing is faster, smoother and more intuitive than ever.

Designed for versatility, the belt can be used when paddling out, heading back in, or anytime you want to keep your hands clear. A side leash connection point allows the belt to twist naturally around your body, so it can be worn comfortably at the front or the back depending on your preference.

The new version also features an integrated internal pocket and a more anatomic, ergonomic fit, improving comfort during long sessions. Built with upgraded, higher-quality materials, the D-Wing Belt V2 is lighter, more durable and more functional, perfectly suited to the demands of parawinging.

More practical and performance-driven, the Flow D-Wing Belt V2 is a simple but essential upgrade that makes every session easier and more enjoyable.
