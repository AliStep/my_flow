DESCRIPTION

The Mullet2 blends the sweet handling and efficiency of the original Mullet with the extra dive, speed and energy retention developed in the Mullet X, bringing these characteristics together into one refined wing.

It represents the culmination of the experience gained while developing our latest generation of parakites, integrating everything we’ve learned into a single design.

At its core is our proven reflex airfoil, already used across our other parakites, but further refined to increase efficiency while maintaining excellent collapse resistance and outstanding pitch stability. The result is a wing with beautifully smooth handling, impressive dive, strong speed range and exceptional energy retention.

One of the defining qualities of the Mullet2 is its very broad usable wind range. Its improved efficiency allows riders to use the same size wing in lighter winds and still generate enough lift to soar. At the same time, the wing dives much more effectively with hands up, allowing it to comfortably handle stronger winds as well.

This wide performance envelope makes it equally at home soaring coastal cliffs in strong wind or flying dynamic terrain in the mountains, following the contours of ridges and peaks.

Despite its performance, the Mullet2 remains approachable and predictable. The wing has a very well-defined stall point and a calm, well-behaved character that makes it easy to manage.

This makes it suitable for a wide range of pilots, from riders entering the parakite sport to highly experienced pilots looking to push the limits with dynamic flying and loops.

Durability was also a key design goal. The canopy uses Skytex 38 fabric, paired with Spectra lines and Dyneema risers, creating a robust and long-lasting wing capable of withstanding heavy use over multiple seasons.

The combination of efficiency, range, durability and versatility is what defines the Mullet2. Its performance spans multiple disciplines and conditions, making it equally capable in light winds, strong winds, coastal soaring or mountain flying.

For these reasons, the Mullet2 stands as our ultimate and most versatile parakite.

REFLEX PROFILE

Reflex Profile – Refined Performance & Unmatched Stability

The Mullet2 features our latest, efficient reflex profile, representing one of the key performance features behind its exceptional solidity, efficiency and safety.

Building on the foundation of the original profile, this updated design has been refined to deliver better aerodynamic efficiency and improved glide, without compromising the core characteristics that define the Mullet series. Pitch stability remains rock-solid, and the wing retains its strong resistance to collapse – a key feature of the Flow parakite range.

The result is a profile that allows pilots to confidently explore higher speeds and deeper dives, while maintaining a reassuringly stable and controlled feel. Even in gusty, turbulent air the wing demonstrates an exceptional level of structural integrity, reinforcing the trust pilots place in it.

This balance of efficiency, performance and inherent safety is what makes the Mullet2 profile truly shine – pushing performance forward while and preserving the confidence pilots rely on.

RISERS AND TIP STEERING

Advanced Riser System

A big part of the Mullet 2’s magic lies in its redesigned riser system.

For this generation, we introduced a new pulley geometry that increases travel, allowing the wing to generate more dive and higher speed with the same amplitude of hand movement,  delivered in an exceptionally smooth and predictable way.

The result is a glider that accelerates progressively and intuitively, giving pilots full confidence when pushing performance.

Brake pressure has been carefully balanced  – not too light, not too heavy – providing the perfect amount of feedback so pilots can precisely refine their lines and carve turns with accuracy.

For the first time in our parakite range, the Mullet 2 also features 2D tip steering. Commonly found on high-performance paramotor wings with reflex profiles, this system allows for sharper, more dynamic turns, including pivot-style manoeuvres, while maintaining stability.

The result is a wing that feels more agile, more responsive, and opens up an entirely new dimension of control – adding another layer to the already immersive 3D flying experience.
